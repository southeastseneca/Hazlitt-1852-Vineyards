About

Hazlitt 1852 Vineyards

Hazlitt 1852 Vineyards is one of the most beloved destinations on Seneca Lake — a family winery that has been farming the same land on the eastern shore since 1852, now celebrating 40 years of commercial winemaking. Founded in 1985 by the late Jerry and Elaine Hazlitt, the winery is now managed by sixth-generation family members Doug Hazlitt and Leigh Hazlitt Triner.

The winery is home to the world-famous Red Cat, one of New York State's top-selling wines, and an award-winning portfolio that includes Riesling, Gewürztraminer, Cabernet Franc, Pinot Gris, Vidal Blanc Ice Wine, and the Cider Tree hard cider line. The tasting room — named one of the Top 25 in the United States by Wine Enthusiast Magazine — is built around the legendary horseshoe bar, antique decor, and the smell of fresh popcorn that greets every visitor.

Outside, The Oasis outdoor pavilion comes alive seasonally for live music, dancing under the stars, and leisurely afternoons on the patio overlooking sparkling ponds, rolling horse pastures, and vineyard views. It's a true Finger Lakes institution — casual, welcoming, and impossible to visit just once.

In Our Words

Our Story

Hazlitt is one of those places that earns its reputation every single time. The horseshoe bar is everything it's cracked up to be — warm, lively, and staffed by people who genuinely love talking about the wine. The Red Cat is a regional icon for good reason, and the Riesling is seriously impressive for anyone who thinks they know what Finger Lakes whites taste like.

On a summer Friday evening at The Oasis with live music playing, a glass of something cold, and those vineyard views stretching out in every direction — this is what the Finger Lakes is all about. Six generations of family have poured their hearts into this land, and you taste it in every bottle. Don't leave without a Red Cat to take home.

— South East Seneca

The Wines

What's Pouring

From the iconic Red Cat to award-winning Riesling and a premium hard cider, Hazlitt has something for every palate. Tastings available at the horseshoe bar daily.

Red Cat ★ — The Original

One of New York State's best-selling wines and a Finger Lakes icon since 1985. A high-quality sweet red wine made from Catawba grapes — approachable, fun, and impossible not to love. The wine that started it all for the Hazlitt family.

  • RieslingDry to semi-sweet · A flagship white and regional standout
  • GewürztraminerAromatic and expressive · One of the best in the Finger Lakes
  • Cabernet FrancPremium red · Rich, structured, and estate-grown
  • Pinot GrisCrisp and food-friendly · A versatile white for any occasion
  • Vidal Blanc Ice WineDessert wine · Intensely sweet and internationally renowned
  • Cider Tree Hard CiderPremium hard cider · Made from locally grown apples

Plan Your Visit

Tips for Visitors

  • Start with the Red Cat — it's the house icon and a great introduction to what Hazlitt is all about.
  • Friday live music runs 5:30–8:30 PM at The Oasis — extended hours, a great evening stop.
  • The Oasis is open seasonally Memorial Day weekend through end of September — check Facebook for exact hours.
  • The horseshoe bar is the heart of the experience — sit down, let the staff tell you the story of each wine.
  • The gift shop has Red Cat merchandise, wine club options, and bottles to ship home.
  • Open year-round except Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's Day, and Easter.
